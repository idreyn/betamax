The Beta opcode demystifier
If you're a student in 6.004, you've probably spent a lot of time picking apart Beta instructions by hand to figure out what they do. Well no more! You can use this tool to convert opcodes between hex and binary, examine different parts of each opcode, and hopefully gain some intuition along the way. Don't have too much fun.
